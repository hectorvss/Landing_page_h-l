Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we’re most often asked before a project starts.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?
We typically work with businesses that deal with repetitive communication, lead flow, bookings, support or operational complexity — including clinics, ecommerce, agencies, real estate, SaaS and service businesses.
Do you only build automations, or also larger systems?
We work across multiple levels — from quick workflow automations and AI assistants to voice agents, operational systems and tailored software products.
How is pricing determined?
Smaller and more standardized services can be priced more specifically, while larger systems depend on integrations, logic, channels, complexity and customization.
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and support for automations, AI assistants, voice systems and larger custom builds, depending on the project scope.
Can you build something custom for our business?
Yes. If your business needs something beyond standard automations, we can design a tailored workflow system, internal tool, client portal or larger software product.
How long does a typical project take?
Simple automations can be delivered relatively quickly, while assistants, voice systems and larger custom builds depend on complexity, integrations and review cycles.
Can you work with our existing tools?
In most cases, yes. Projects are often built around existing tools, CRMs, communication channels and operational workflows already used by the business.
What happens before a project starts?
We usually begin by understanding the current process, identifying bottlenecks and defining the right scope before proposing the solution structure.
Do you use AI in every project?
Not always. We use AI where it adds real value. Some systems are better solved with pure automation and logic, while others benefit from assistants, classification or voice layers.
What if we are not sure what we need yet?
That is common. We can help map the right level of solution — whether that is a small workflow, an assistant, a voice agent or a more tailored system.
